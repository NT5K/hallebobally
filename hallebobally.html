WHAT IS HBB?

Halle Bo Bally is an interactive experience that includes not only story book reading, but songs and dance too! HBB story telling sparks imagination, while music and movement help to build coordination and confidence. Kids learn best through play, right?!

MISSION

Your child's early years are so important for development. At HBB, we nurture little minds and hearts by fostering a love of reading, music and movement. Our goal is to help build social skills through group interactions and to encourage creativity and self-expression through play.

AN EXPERIENCE FOR ALL!

HBB isn't just an experience for your child -- it's meant to be fun for YOU too! Bringing your child to HBB is a great way for you to bond, while they learn and play with other kids. You'll make memories laughing together at the stories, singing along to the songs, and dancing to the rhythms!
