What is it?

Fun and learning through stories, songs and dances Kids learn best through play! Our interactive storytimes, songs and dances provide a magical experience where kids can learn through having fun.

We read stories that spark their imagination, sing songs that help develop language skills and lead dances that build coordination and confidence.

My Mission

Nurturing little minds and hearts Your child's early years are so important for development. At our storytimes and sessions, we nurture little minds and hearts by fostering a love of reading, music and movement. We help build social skills through group interactions and encourage creativity and self-expression through stories and play.

Experience

Bond with your child through shared experiences There's nothing more special than sharing experiences with your little one. Bringing your child to our storytimes and sessions is a great way for you to bond while they learn and play with other kids. You'll make memories together laughing at the stories, singing along to the songs and dancing to the rhythms.

Any Occasion

Discover new books and find storytime favorites Kids love familiarity and repetition! At our story sessions, we read both new books to spark curiosity as well as storytime favorites your little one will want to hear again and again. We curate a selection of the best children's books to help develop your child's literacy skills in a fun and engaging way.
