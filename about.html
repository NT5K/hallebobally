About

Originally from Cleveland, Ohio, I received my undergrad from Malone University in Communication Arts with a minor in Marketing. Having attended university on a vocal performance scholarship, I have been on stages both locally and internationally, including being a soloist in Italy.

I have enjoyed a colorful career life - sales, PR agency work, and motivational speaking for middle & high school students. My performance background includes public speaking, modeling, theater, television, voice over, and directing. I have recorded cast tracks for up and coming musicals, as well as recorded at the famous Gaither Studios.

Currently, I keep myself in a chaotic spin of Halle Bo Bally appearances, princess party engagements, theatrical contracts, jazz and wedding contracts, commercial and VO work, and directing responsibilities as the Director of the St. Edward High School's Edwardian Players.
