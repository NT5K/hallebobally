About

Originally from Cleveland, Ohio, I received my undergrad from Malone University in Communication Arts with a minor in Marketing. Having attended university on a vocal performance scholarship, I have been on stages both locally and internationally, including being a soloist in Italy.

I have enjoyed a colorful career life - sales, PR agency work, and motivational speaking for middle & high school students. My performance background includes public speaking, modeling, theater, television and voice over. I have recorded cast tracks for up and coming musicals, as well as recorded at the famous Gaither Studios.

Currently, I keep myself in a chaotic spin of Halle Bo Bally appearances, princess party engagements, theatrical contracts, jazz and wedding contracts, commercial and voice over work, and acting coaching.
